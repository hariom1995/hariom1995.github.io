About Me

Hello, my name is Hariom Mukati!! I'm a designer and front
end developer from Bhopal,India. Currently working at India.

I specialize in designing and developing user interfaces.
My attention to detail is extreme and I love to continually learn what's new
all things design-related.

gym or in the my bedroom.
